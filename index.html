About me

My name is Moritz (or just Moe) and I am a Software Engineer from Germany.

During my studies at the University of Stuttgart I achieved a Bachelors degree in Control Science, which was followed by a Master degree in Computer Sciences. This allowed me to gain insights in a wide array of fields, including Embedded Systems, Control Algorithms, Machine Learning and Robotics. Concluded were these studies with a Bachelor thesis on the Control System of a real satellite and a Master thesis in which I implemented an framework for the adaptive compression scientific simulation data.

If I’m not hacking away on my side projects or trying to find the next big thing, you are most likely find me in the gym, climbing mountains or catching waves.
